Purpose

Hi, I’m Marie. I’m new to graphic and web design but I’m passionate at what I do. I design with a purpose in mind and that purpose will always be how to help your business. If you’re a small business owner who can’t afford a seasoned designer, I can help you. Tell about me about your problems and I can design solutions and get results.

Check out my projects and let me know if you like what you see.

Audience

My target audience is small business owners who are in need of reaching out to more customers through a website or other graphic design needs. These entrepreneurs do not have the capacity to hire highly experienced designers. They are looking for an affordable designer, willing to take a chance on a less experienced designer but not compromise on quality and strong design.

About

Hi, I'm Marie.

I fell in love with graphic design ever since I watched a documentary on how Hallmark made their greeting cards. The design process involved really captivated me. I told myself then, "I want to do that." Life took me on a different path but 23 years later I'm trying to live my first passion. I'm currently studying Web Design and Development at Brigham Young University Idaho. I learn and refine my skills by working on school projects and other personal projects.

I was born and raise in the Philippines but now lives in Utah with my husband and 4 little humans. I enjoy doing anything my children loves to do: playing Minecraft, going to pars, even singing Baby Shark for the hundredth time.

My Design Philosophy
I take the design process seriously. I love seeing one small idea turn into something amazing. Of course, there are many steps involved in growing a small seed of idea into something fruitful. There's research, ideation, sketching, collaboration, feedback, testing, and of course, more feedback.
