About Provo River Canyon

The Provo River Canyon is a Class I-II rapids which means it's fun for everyone. It has easy rapids with smaller waves and clear channels that are obvious without scouting. Paddle down the river with friends and family and experience a 6-mile journey through the amazing views of soaring canyon walls of the Provo River Canyon.

Choose Guided Rafting Adventure that comes with a boat and a guide or be your own guide with a Self-Guided Rafting Adventure.
